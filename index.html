Why Choose Us

What Sets
Us Apart

⬡

Commercial-Grade Materials

We use the same high-build epoxy and coating systems trusted in industrial facilities — not hardware store kits. Every pour is engineered for long-term performance, not just a good first impression.

✦

Every Job Is Custom

No packages, no guesswork. Every project starts with a free site visit, a proper surface assessment, and a finish plan built around your space, your use case, and your budget.

◈

Surface Prep Is Everything

Most coating failures come from inadequate preparation. We diamond-grind every slab before application — ensuring the coating bonds correctly and performs as long as it's designed to.

★

20-Year Workmanship Guarantee

We stand behind every floor we install. If the coating fails due to our application, we come back and fix it at no cost. Our reputation is built project by project — and we intend to keep it that way.
